
*Introduction:**

Mujhe apna fyp ka projecct proposal banana hai toh main batadeta hun project main hoga kia.

Project ye hai k fyp ka kaam university main sara manual hota ai toh usko automate krna hai or usko access kereingay students, admin(committee team) and supervisors.

Ab uske lye humein 3 panel bnane prenge students ka alag, FYP committee team ka alag or supervisor ka alag taake sbko unke kaam k hisab se content show hojaye or manual working se automated work ki trf shift hojaein sab.

Isse students ko bhi easy rhega k aik he platform pr sab kaam download or upload krhe honge and committee members or supervisors k lye bhi easy hojaega k sab kaam website se he manage krlenge.





**Manual Working(Current):**

**Student:**

* Abhi aese horha k fyp team manually sbse Pehle tou bachon k groups bnate hain form submission krwakr usme bache apna project idea, members and supervisor likhte or phr university usko aik document bnakr rkhti hain or baad main usme status daldeti hai presentation k baad k Kiska idea approve hua Kiska reject hua.
* Project proposal bhi hardcopy main fyp team ko submit hota.
* Meeting logs and meeting minutes bhi hardcopy main student fill krha hota then supervisor se share krha hota.
* Project report bhi hardcopy main submit hoti.



**Admin:**

* Project accept ya reject excel sheet pr pta chlta hai or WhatsApp pr fyp team share krdeti students k sath.
* Marks wagera bhi jo dete presentation or report wagera k woh bhi hardcopy main derhe hote.
* Koi bhi cheez btani ho ya announcements krni ho tou students ko WhatsApp group pr message krke btate hain.



**Supervisor:**

* Student ko meeting log and meeting minutes hard copy main submit krwane prte hain supervisor ko every week throughout the semester.
* Marks ki evaluation bhi supervisor ko hard copy main krni prti hai.





**Automation:**

**students:**

* Student ayega website pr apna project idea dalega, supervisor name dalega or apne members name dalega or presentation k baad fyp team ya admin usme status likhde website pr aake he k Kiska project approve hua Kiska reject.
* Project proposal form submit krega.
* Meeting logs main meeting ki detail dalega har hafte, total 14 meeting logs hote aik semester main.
* Project report submit krega semester end main.



**Admin:**

* Project accept / reject hua wo bateyga website pr he.
* Students k marks upload krega website per(presentation, sessional, report, meeting logs etc).
* Website pr he banner bnkr ajaein announcement k, admin woh announcement apne portal se appload krega phr har student k portal pr show hojaegi woh announcement.



**Supervisor:**

* Meeting logs and meeting minutes ko website pr he fill krke submit krdena hoga taake website se he supervisor check krle submission students ki.
* Marks ki evaluation bhi website pr he krde supervisor or phr students ko show hojaein marks but after the announcement of overall fyp result.





**Project Hierarchy:**

Three parts main divide hoga project

1. **Project proposal**
2. **FYP-1**
3. **FYP-2**



**Project Proposal:**

1. Project proposal form submit krega student.
2. Then proposal ki marking k baad aik award list bnegi(1st, 2nd and 3rd) position ki markings ki base pr.
3. Award List\_1 show hojaegi website pr.

**Note:** Project proposal and FYP-1 dono aik he semester(like 7th semester) main honge means FYP-1 main dono kaam honge yeh then uske baad FYP-2(like 8th semester) ayega.



**FYP-1(100 marks):**

1. **Presentation (40):**
Yeh 40 marks ki hogi or iski evaluation fyp committee members krenge.
2. **Report (report (20) + demonstration (10)):**
Yeh total 30 marks ki hai 20+10 main split krdia hai bss.
Iski evaluation 2 log(fyp committee member and faculty member) krte and then cumulative marks nikalte dono members ki marking k.
3. **Sessional (20):**
Iski marking supervisor krta hai.
4. **Meeting logs (10):**
Iski marking committee member krta.
5. **Award List\_2:**

&#x20;  Pura FYP-1 ki marking k baad aik award list\_2 bnegi(1st, 2nd and 3rd) position ki markings ki base pr then award list show hojaegi web pe.

**Note:** Yeh har cheez ki marking website pr he hogi. 

&#x20;  

**FYP-2(100 Marks):**

1. **Presentation (40):**
Yeh 40 marks ki hogi or iski evaluation fyp committee members krenge.
2. **Report (25):**
Iski evaluation 2 log(fyp committee member and faculty member) krte and then cumulative marks nikalte dono members ki marking k.
3. **Project demonstration (25):**
Fyp committee and external evaluator marking krega.
4. **Meeting logs (10):**
5 marks ki marking supervisor krta and 5 marks ki marking committee member.
5. **Award List\_3:**

&#x20;  Pura FYP-2 ki marking k baad aik award list\_3 bnegi(1st, 2nd and 3rd) position ki markings ki base pr then award list show hojaegi web pe.

**Note:** Yeh har cheez ki marking website pr he hogi.
